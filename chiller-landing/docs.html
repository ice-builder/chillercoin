$CHILLER Documentation

Technical whitepaper — how the vault works, fees, security, and risks.

Overview

$CHILLER is a vault share token on Solana. Users deposit SOL into the vault and receive $CHILLER tokens representing their share. An AI trading engine (Soldier) trades 24/7 on perpetual futures, growing the vault's total assets. When users withdraw, their $CHILLER is burned and SOL is returned at the current Net Asset Value (NAV).

Key Principle $CHILLER uses a dynamic mint/burn model — there is no fixed supply. Tokens are minted on deposit and burned on withdrawal. The price of $CHILLER reflects the vault's performance.

Vault Model

Deposit Flow

👤 User
→ SOL →
🏦 Vault
→ mint →
🧊 $CHILLER

When you deposit SOL, the vault calculates how many $CHILLER tokens to mint based on the current NAV:

tokens_minted = deposit_amount × total_supply / total_assets

If the vault has 100 SOL in assets and 100 $CHILLER supply, depositing 10 SOL mints 10 $CHILLER. If the vault grew to 200 SOL with 100 supply, depositing 10 SOL mints only 5 $CHILLER (because each token is now worth 2 SOL).

Withdraw Flow

👤 User
→ $CHILLER →
🔥 Burn
→ SOL →
💰 Receive

When you withdraw, $CHILLER tokens are burned and SOL is returned at NAV:

sol_returned = tokens_burned × total_assets / total_supply

NAV Calculation

NAV (Net Asset Value) per token is updated regularly:

NAV = total_assets / total_supply

total_assets includes SOL in the vault + assets on exchanges (reported by the trading engine). NAV updates happen every 24 hours via an authorized oracle call.

AI Trading Engine

Soldier Bot

The Soldier is our proprietary AI trading engine that runs 24/7. It combines multiple strategies:

  • IIE (Impulse-Inertia Engine) — detects momentum impulses and trades the follow-through
  • Z-Score Scanner — finds mean-reversion opportunities across 245 coins
  • Macro Bias — adjusts directional exposure based on BTC market structure
  • Adaptive Position Manager — ML-based position sizing using historical performance

Fee Structure

FeeRateDescription
Performance Fee20%Charged on realized profit above High Water Mark (HWM). Team only earns when vault grows.
Management Fee2% / yearAnnual fee on total assets, accrued daily.
Withdrawal Fee0.5%Small fee to prevent flash deposit/withdraw attacks.
High Water Mark (HWM) The performance fee only applies to new profits. If the vault drops from 100 SOL to 90 SOL, no performance fee is charged until the vault recovers past 100 SOL. This protects depositors from paying fees during drawdowns.

Security

Squads Multisig

The vault authority will be transferred to a Squads 2-of-3 multisig. This means no single person can modify the vault parameters, withdraw funds, or change the authority. Any admin action requires 2 out of 3 team members to approve.

Emergency Pause

The vault has an emergency pause function that instantly freezes all deposits and withdrawals. This can be triggered by any multisig signer to protect funds in case of an exploit or market emergency.

Additional safety measures:

  • Daily withdrawal cap — limits total withdrawals per epoch to prevent bank runs
  • Minimum deposit — 0.5 SOL to prevent dust attacks
  • On-chain trade logging — every trade is recorded in the vault's state
  • Open source contract — full source code available on GitHub

Risks

⚠️ Important Crypto trading involves significant risk. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Only deposit what you can afford to lose.
  • Smart contract risk — despite audits, bugs can exist in any code
  • Market risk — AI trading can have losing periods, reducing NAV
  • Oracle risk — NAV depends on off-chain asset reporting
  • Liquidity risk — withdrawal caps may delay large exits
  • Regulatory risk — crypto regulations are evolving
