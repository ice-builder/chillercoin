Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before chilling with us

What is $CHILLER?
+

$CHILLER is a vault share token. When you deposit SOL, the vault mints $CHILLER tokens for you. Our AI bots trade 24/7, and if they profit — your $CHILLER becomes worth more. When you withdraw, the vault burns your tokens and returns SOL at the current NAV (Net Asset Value).

How is $CHILLER different from meme coins?
+

Meme coins have a fixed supply and trade on DEXes — their price is pure speculation. $CHILLER has a dynamic supply backed by real SOL. Price = NAV = actual assets in the vault. No speculation, no rug pulls. Every token is backed by real assets.

How do I deposit?
+

Connect your Phantom wallet on our Dashboard, enter the amount of SOL you want to deposit (minimum 0.5 SOL), and confirm the transaction. The vault automatically mints $CHILLER tokens to your wallet at the current NAV price.

How do I withdraw?
+

Click "Withdraw" on the Dashboard, enter how many $CHILLER tokens to cash out. The vault burns your tokens and sends SOL to your wallet at the current NAV price minus a small 0.5% withdrawal fee. No lock-up periods — withdraw anytime.

What is NAV?
+

Net Asset Value — the price of 1 $CHILLER token. It's calculated as: total vault assets ÷ total token supply. If the bots profit, NAV goes up. If they lose, NAV goes down. NAV is updated every hour and fully transparent on the Dashboard.

Can $CHILLER go to zero?
+

Only if the trading bots lost 100% of all assets — which is virtually impossible due to stop-losses on every trade (max drawdown ~10-15%). Unlike meme coins where a whale dump can crash price to zero, $CHILLER price = real assets. Even in the worst case, emergency pause freezes everything.

What are the fees?
+

Performance Fee: 20% of profits only (High Water Mark — team earns nothing until vault exceeds its previous peak).
Management Fee: 2% annually.
Withdrawal Fee: 0.5% per withdrawal.
No deposit fees. No hidden charges.

What is High Water Mark?
+

Protection for you. The team takes 20% performance fee ONLY from NEW profits above the previous maximum NAV. If the vault drops from $1.15 to $1.05, the team earns $0 in performance fees until NAV exceeds $1.15 again. This aligns our incentives with yours.

Is the smart contract audited?
+

The contract is minimal (270 lines of Rust) and has passed comprehensive integration tests. Automated security audit via Sec3 is planned, followed by a community audit. The contract source will be fully verified and public on Solana Explorer.

What if something goes wrong?
+

The vault has an emergency pause function that instantly freezes all deposits and withdrawals. Daily withdrawal cap limits exposure. Every trade is logged on-chain for full transparency. We're building towards multisig authority for additional security.

How does the team make money?
+

We earn 20% of trading profits only. If the bots don't profit — we earn exactly $0. We do NOT earn from pumping token price and dumping on holders. There's no pre-minted supply to dump. We make money only one way: our bots trade well. Our incentives are 100% aligned with yours.

Do you promise returns?
+

No. We don't promise anything. We built AI trading bots, they work 24/7, and we invite you to join the ride. If the bots earn — you earn. If they don't — you don't. No hype, no fake promises, no "guaranteed 100x". We're just a team of traders who prefer to chill. 🧊

What wallet do I need?
+

You need a Solana-compatible wallet. We recommend Phantom — it's free, available as a Chrome extension and mobile app (iOS/Android). Other options include Solflare, Backpack, or a Ledger hardware wallet for maximum security.

How do I buy $CHILLER?
+

Step 1: Install Phantom wallet from phantom.app.
Step 2: Get SOL — buy on any exchange and send to your Phantom, or buy directly in Phantom with a card.
Step 3: Visit our Dashboard and click "Connect Wallet".
Step 4: Click "Deposit", enter your SOL amount, confirm in Phantom. Done — $CHILLER appears in your wallet! 🧊

Can I send $CHILLER to someone else?
+

Yes! $CHILLER is a standard SPL token on Solana. You can freely send, receive, and store it on any Solana-compatible wallet. However, the only way to redeem it for its real value (NAV) is through the vault's withdraw function on our Dashboard.

My balance is shown in SOL — why not USD?
+

The vault operates in SOL for maximum security — SOL cannot be frozen by any third party. Your Dashboard always shows both: your SOL balance and its USD equivalent in real-time. Keep in mind that your returns depend on two factors: bot trading performance AND SOL price movement.
